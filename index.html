Install AL Software Dashboard

Get app-like experience on your device

For iOS / iPadOS

  1. Open this website in Safari browser
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom or top
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

For Android

  1. Open this website in Chrome browser
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap "Add" or "Install" to confirm
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

For Desktop (Windows / Mac)

  1. Open this website in Chrome or Edge browser
  2. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
  3. Click the icon and then click "Install"
  4. The app will open in its own window
  5. Find it in your Start Menu (Windows) or Applications (Mac)
